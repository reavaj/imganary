# Blurred

Motion smear from long exposure or intentional camera movement — Sugimoto's theaters, Richter's paintings, light trails.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Compresses extended time into a single frame through long exposure, smearing moving elements into streaks while static elements remain sharp (or everything blurs with camera movement).
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Moving elements smeared into streaks or ghosts
  - Static elements remain sharp (tripod) or everything blurs (handheld)
  - Time compressed — minutes or hours in one frame
  - Light trails from moving sources
  - Ghostly, ethereal, time-aware quality
- **Reference points:** Hiroshi Sugimoto (theater interiors), Gerhard Richter (photo-paintings), ICM photography, car light trails, waterfall long exposures
- **Pairs well with:** ethereal, impressionistic, soft-focus, nostalgic

## FLUX keywords
motion blur, blurred, long exposure, light trails, streaked, slow shutter, ghostly blur, movement blur, time lapse blur, ICM photography
