# Flickering

Unstable, pulsing, intermittent light suggesting early cinema or candlelight — silent film projection, strobe, old TV.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Introduces temporal instability to the light source, creating a sense of unreliable, pulsing illumination that suggests mechanical failure, primitive technology, or supernatural presence.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Light source unstable, pulsing, intermittent
  - Frame-rate artifacts or strobe effect
  - Haunted, primitive, mechanical quality
  - Suggests old projectors, candles, failing electronics
  - Unease through visual instability
- **Reference points:** Silent film projection flicker, candlelit scenes, strobe light photography, old CRT television static, early Edison films
- **Pairs well with:** candlelit, nostalgic, eerie, grainy

## FLUX keywords
flickering, strobe, pulsing light, unstable light, old film, projector flicker, intermittent, stroboscopic, unsteady, stuttering light
