# Frozen

Crisp, perfectly stopped motion capturing a single instant — Muybridge, Edgerton's bullet, flash photography.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Stops time absolutely, capturing a split-second that the eye could never see — suspended droplets, mid-air leaps, shattering glass — with zero motion blur.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Zero motion blur, absolute sharpness
  - Impossible moments made visible
  - Suspended animation, time literally stopped
  - Often reveals hidden physics (splashes, shatters)
  - Flash or high-speed shutter implied
- **Reference points:** Eadweard Muybridge (horse in motion), Harold Edgerton (bullet through apple, milk drop), flash photography, sports photography peak action
- **Pairs well with:** hyper-detailed, photorealistic, harsh, deep-focus

## FLUX keywords
frozen motion, stopped action, freeze frame, high speed photography, frozen in time, split second, suspended, flash frozen, motion captured, instant frozen
