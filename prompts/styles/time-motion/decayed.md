# Decayed

Time's erosion made visible — ruined frescoes, daguerreotype foxing, abandoned buildings, memento mori.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Shows the cumulative destructive effect of time on materials — rot, ruin, disintegration, biological invasion — transforming human creation back toward entropy.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Visible deterioration and material breakdown
  - Peeling, crumbling, staining, biological growth
  - Former glory implied through remaining fragments
  - Beauty in destruction (ruin aesthetics)
  - Memento mori — reminder of impermanence
- **Reference points:** Ruined frescoes (Pompeii), daguerreotype foxing and deterioration, abandoned buildings (Pripyat), Piranesi's ruins, Caspar David Friedrich (Abbey in the Oakwood)
- **Pairs well with:** weathered, corroded, cracked, desolate

## FLUX keywords
decayed, decaying, rotting, ruined, crumbling, deteriorating, aged, time-worn, entropy, abandoned decay, beautiful ruin, memento mori
