# Anime Cel Animation

The hand-painted cel animation aesthetic of 1980s-90s anime — Studio Ghibli, Akira, Ghost in the Shell. Rich, detailed backgrounds with clean character art.

## Visual DNA
- **Lighting:** Dramatic rim lighting on characters, atmospheric haze. Golden hour and blue hour heavily used. Light rays through dust particles. Strong color in shadows (purple, blue).
- **Composition:** Cinematic widescreen framing. Layered parallax depth — detailed foreground, character mid-ground, painterly background. Dynamic action lines for movement.
- **Color palette:** Rich and saturated. Warm ambers and cool teals. Sunset gradients. Night scenes with deep indigo and neon accent lighting.
- **Textures & materials:** Smooth cel-shaded characters (flat color, hard shadow edges) against painterly gouache backgrounds. Visible paint strokes in skies and foliage.
- **Mood:** Epic, wistful, or kinetic. A sense of vast worlds and personal journeys.
- **Rendering:** Clean ink outlines on characters, no outlines on backgrounds. 2-3 tone shading (base, shadow, highlight). Hair and fabric with flowing dynamic curves.
