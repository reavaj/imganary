# Vector Futurism

Vector Futurism is a visually striking art style blending the utopian ideals of early 20th-century Futurism with modern vector graphics techniques. It evokes a sense of speed, technology, and progress, often depicting sleek, streamlined forms and dynamic compositions. The style marries the optimism of the future with a clean, graphic sensibility.

## Visual DNA
- **Lighting:** Bright, flat lighting with gradients to suggest form and motion; occasional use of glowing neon effects.
- **Composition:** Dynamic, diagonal lines and overlapping shapes suggesting movement; asymmetrical layouts that convey speed and energy; frequent use of perspective to emphasize depth and scale.
- **Color palette:** Bold, primary colors (red, yellow, blue) alongside metallic hues (silver, gold) and occasional use of neon colors; high contrast between colors to enhance visual impact.
- **Textures & materials:** Smooth, polished surfaces; metallic finishes; transparent or translucent elements suggesting advanced technology; minimal texture overall, emphasizing clean lines.
- **Mood:** Optimistic, energetic, futuristic, technologically advanced, sleek, and efficient.
- **Rendering:** Flat vector graphics with gradients; sharp, clean lines; geometric shapes; stylized representations of objects and figures.
