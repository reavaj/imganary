# Microscopic

  - Cellular, crystalline, molecular structures
  - Often symmetrical or repeating patterns
- **Reference points:** Electron microscopy, Ernst Haeckel (Art Forms in Nature), macro photography (insects, crystals), medical imaging, microphotography competitions
- **Pairs well with:** intricate, hyper-detailed, chromatic, geometric
