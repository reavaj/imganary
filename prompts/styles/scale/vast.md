# Vast

Extreme sense of scale, viewer dwarfed by environment — Caspar David Friedrich, IMAX nature films, space photography.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Establishes an overwhelming sense of environmental scale where human figures become specks and the landscape or space itself becomes the dominant subject.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Tiny figures in enormous landscapes
  - Sky or environment dominates 80%+ of frame
  - Sublime scale — beautiful and terrifying
  - Distance and depth feel infinite
  - Human insignificance against nature or cosmos
- **Reference points:** Caspar David Friedrich (Wanderer Above the Sea of Fog), IMAX nature documentaries, space photography (Pale Blue Dot), Thomas Cole (Hudson River School)
- **Pairs well with:** panoramic, desolate, monumental, serene

## FLUX keywords
vast, enormous, epic scale, sweeping, immense, endless, grand vista, tiny figure in landscape, overwhelming scale, infinite expanse
