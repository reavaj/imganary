# Intricate

Dense, fine, complex detail rewarding close inspection — illuminated manuscripts, Islamic tile, Fabergé eggs, Escher.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Packs the image with fine, interconnected detail at multiple scales, creating visual complexity that reveals new information the closer you look.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Detail within detail within detail (fractal-like)
  - Fine elements interconnected and interlocking
  - Rewards close inspection with new discoveries
  - Obsessive precision and patience visible
  - Pattern, repetition, and variation at tiny scale
- **Reference points:** Illuminated manuscripts (Book of Kells), Islamic geometric tilework, Fabergé eggs, M.C. Escher, Indian miniature painting
- **Pairs well with:** ornate, hyper-detailed, geometric, sinuous

## FLUX keywords
intricate, intricate detail, complex patterns, fine detail, elaborate, delicate detail, detailed ornament, filigree, dense pattern, meticulous
