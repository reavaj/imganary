# Ornate

Excessive decorative detail, every surface embellished — Rococo interiors, Victorian frames, Versailles, Gothic cathedrals.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Covers every available surface with decoration, carving, gilding, and ornament, leaving nothing plain and creating a sense of lavish, overwhelming decorative excess.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Every surface decorated — nothing left bare
  - Scrollwork, carving, gilding, filigree
  - Multiple decorative systems layered together
  - Horror vacui applied to ornament
  - Craftsmanship displayed through excess
- **Reference points:** Rococo interiors (Amalienburg), Victorian picture frames, Versailles (Hall of Mirrors), Gothic cathedral facades, Indian Jain temples
- **Pairs well with:** opulent, intricate, jewel-toned, burnished

## FLUX keywords
ornate, decorated, embellished, gilded, scrollwork, baroque decoration, elaborate ornament, richly decorated, carved detail, gold trim
