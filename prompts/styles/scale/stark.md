# Stark

Stripped bare, high contrast, nothing soft or forgiving — black and white photography, Bergman, winter landscapes.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Removes all softness, middle tones, and comfort from the image, leaving only the raw extremes — black and white, empty and full, present and absent.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Extreme contrast, minimal midtones
  - Nothing hidden or softened
  - Raw, exposed, unforgiving honesty
  - Often monochrome or near-monochrome
  - Emotional impact through visual severity
- **Reference points:** Ingmar Bergman (The Seventh Seal), black and white photography, winter landscapes, Anton Corbijn, brutalist architecture against sky
- **Pairs well with:** harsh, graphic, austere, monochromatic

## FLUX keywords
stark, bare, stripped, high contrast, raw, exposed, unforgiving, black and white, brutal, nothing hidden
