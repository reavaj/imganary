# Coarse

Large, rough, unrefined detail lacking finesse — burlap texture, rough-hewn stone, outsider art, brutalist aggregate.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Enlarges the grain of texture and detail to a chunky, unpolished scale, rejecting refinement in favor of raw, physical, material presence.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Large, chunky, visible texture elements
  - Unrefined, rough, unfinished surfaces
  - Raw material presence (stone, concrete, wood grain)
  - Anti-precision, anti-polish
  - Tactile, heavy, grounded in physical matter
- **Reference points:** Brutalist concrete aggregate, burlap and hessian, rough-hewn timber, outsider art (Jean Dubuffet), dry stone walls
- **Pairs well with:** gritty, matte, austere, stark

## FLUX keywords
coarse, rough, chunky, unrefined, raw, rough-hewn, heavy texture, brutal, crude, thick grain
