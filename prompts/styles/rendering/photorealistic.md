# Photorealistic

Indistinguishable from a photograph, hyperreal precision — Chuck Close's pores, CGI VFX, trompe l'oeil painting.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Renders every detail with such fidelity that the image appears to be a direct optical capture of reality, achieving uncanny accuracy in texture, light, and form.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Every surface texture resolved (pores, fibers, reflections)
  - Physically accurate light behavior
  - No visible style or artistic hand
  - Can trigger uncanny valley when applied to faces
  - Technical perfection as aesthetic goal
- **Reference points:** Chuck Close, CGI (Avatar, The Mandalorian), trompe l'oeil painting, product photography, architectural visualization
- **Pairs well with:** glossy, deep-focus, hyper-detailed, frozen

## FLUX keywords
photorealistic, hyperrealistic, photo-real, ultra-realistic, lifelike, indistinguishable from photo, perfect detail, realistic rendering
