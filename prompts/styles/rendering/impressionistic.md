# Impressionistic

Loose suggestion of form through light and color rather than line — Monet's gardens, Renoir's light, Malick's golden hour.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Prioritizes the fleeting impression of light and atmosphere over precise rendering, using soft edges, visible brushwork, and dissolved forms to capture a moment's essence.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Light and color over line and form
  - Soft, blurred edges and dissolved contours
  - Visible brushwork or motion suggesting spontaneity
  - Outdoor light, especially dappled or reflected
  - Captured moment rather than composed scene
- **Reference points:** Monet (Water Lilies, Impression Sunrise), Renoir, Terrence Malick, motion blur photography, Berthe Morisot
- **Pairs well with:** diffused, painterly, soft-focus, ethereal

## FLUX keywords
impressionistic, Impressionist, loose brushwork, soft edges, light and color, Monet style, blurred forms, captured moment, dappled light
