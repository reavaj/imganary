# Solarized

Partial tonal inversion from the Sabattier darkroom effect — Man Ray's experiments, psychedelic album art, alien landscapes.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Partially reverses the tonal values in highlights or shadows, creating a metallic, otherworldly appearance where light and dark swap in unexpected zones.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Partial tone reversal (lights become dark, darks become light in select areas)
  - Metallic, mercury-like sheen on surfaces
  - Alien, unnatural color relationships
  - Dark outlines (Mackie lines) at tonal boundaries
  - Surreal, chemical, darkroom-accident aesthetic
- **Reference points:** Man Ray (Sabattier experiments), psychedelic album covers, darkroom experimental photography, Lee Miller
- **Pairs well with:** chromatic, hallucinatory, graphic, neon
