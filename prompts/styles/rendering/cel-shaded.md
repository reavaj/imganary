# Cel-shaded

Flat color fills with hard shadow edges, anime/cartoon rendering — Ghibli, Borderlands, Spider-Verse.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Replaces photographic tonal gradation with flat color zones separated by hard-edged shadow boundaries, creating a hand-animated or cartoon appearance.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Flat color fills with no gradient
  - Hard-edged shadow shapes (not soft falloff)
  - Bold outlines defining forms
  - Limited number of tonal steps (2-4 per surface)
  - Clean, animated, hand-drawn appearance
- **Reference points:** Studio Ghibli, Borderlands (game), Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse, classic Disney cel animation, Samurai Jack
- **Pairs well with:** stylized, graphic, saturated, bold

## FLUX keywords
cel-shaded, cel shading, anime style, flat shading, cartoon rendering, animated, hard shadow edge, bold outline, toon shading
