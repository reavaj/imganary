# Sketchy

Loose, unfinished, exploratory line quality — Da Vinci's studies, concept art, architectural sketches.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Preserves the raw, searching energy of preliminary drawing — construction lines visible, forms only partially resolved, capturing the thought process of creation.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Visible construction lines and search marks
  - Forms partially resolved, some areas unfinished
  - Spontaneous, raw, unpolished energy
  - Pencil, charcoal, or ink on paper feel
  - Process visible alongside result
- **Reference points:** Leonardo da Vinci (anatomical studies), concept art (Syd Mead, Ralph McQuarrie), architectural sketches, fashion croquis
- **Pairs well with:** minimal, angular, cross-hatched, monochromatic

## FLUX keywords
sketchy, sketch, rough drawing, loose lines, unfinished, concept art, pencil sketch, quick drawing, gestural lines, preliminary
