# Expressionistic

Form distorted to convey raw emotion over accuracy — Munch's scream, German Expressionist cinema, Francis Bacon's figures.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Warps, stretches, and distorts visual reality to externalize internal emotional states, making the viewer feel the subject's psychological experience.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Distorted proportions and perspective
  - Intense, non-naturalistic color
  - Jagged, aggressive line quality
  - Psychological truth over optical truth
  - Raw, unfiltered emotional energy
- **Reference points:** Edvard Munch (The Scream), German Expressionist cinema (Caligari, Nosferatu), Francis Bacon, Egon Schiele, Käthe Kollwitz
- **Pairs well with:** harsh, angular, saturated, fragmented

## FLUX keywords
expressionistic, Expressionist, distorted, emotional, warped, intense, angular distortion, psychological, raw emotion, Munch style
