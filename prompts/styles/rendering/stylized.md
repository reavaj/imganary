# Stylized

Deliberate departure from realism toward a designed aesthetic — Ghibli's worlds, Art Deco illustration, Pixar's appeal.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Applies a consistent, designed visual language that simplifies and exaggerates reality according to deliberate aesthetic rules, creating a recognizable "look."
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Simplified forms with exaggerated key features
  - Consistent design language throughout
  - Recognizable artistic signature
  - Reality filtered through a design system
  - Appeal through distillation, not accuracy
- **Reference points:** Studio Ghibli, Pixar, Art Deco illustration, Alphonse Mucha, Spider-Verse, Samurai Jack
- **Pairs well with:** cel-shaded, graphic, saturated, organic

## FLUX keywords
stylized, artistic, designed, non-realistic, illustrated, animated style, artistic interpretation, exaggerated, distinctive style
