# Graphic

High-contrast flat shapes with bold outlines, poster-like clarity — Saul Bass, Frank Miller, woodcut prints.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Reduces the image to flat shapes, high contrast, and bold outlines, eliminating tonal subtlety in favor of immediate, poster-like visual impact.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Black and white or limited flat color
  - Bold outlines defining shapes
  - No tonal gradation — flat fills only
  - Immediate read from across the room
  - Print-derived aesthetic (woodcut, linocut, screen print)
- **Reference points:** Saul Bass (film titles), Frank Miller (Sin City), woodcut prints, propaganda posters, Shepard Fairey (Obey)
- **Pairs well with:** stark, silhouetted, halftoned, angular

## FLUX keywords
graphic, bold, high contrast, flat shapes, poster style, Saul Bass, black and white, strong outlines, woodcut, graphic novel
