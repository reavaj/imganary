# Art Deco Architecture

The glamorous geometric excess of 1920s-30s skyscrapers, cinemas, and ocean liners — Chrysler Building, Miami Beach, Shanghai Bund. Luxury expressed through angular symmetry.

## Visual DNA
- **Lighting:** Dramatic uplighting on facades. Warm interior glow through geometric windows. Neon and chrome accents catching light. Night scenes with the building as luminous jewel against dark sky.
- **Composition:** Symmetrical, frontal elevation views. Strong vertical lines tapering upward — ziggurat setbacks. Dramatic forced perspective looking up at towers. Grand entrance portals framing deep interiors.
- **Color palette:** Rich metallics — gold, brass, chrome, bronze. Deep jewel tones — emerald, sapphire, ruby. Black and cream as base. Terracotta, jade green.
- **Textures & materials:** Polished marble, terrazzo, hammered bronze, etched glass, lacquered wood. Geometric inlay patterns — chevrons, sunbursts, fans, zigzags. Chrome railings, mirrored surfaces.
- **Mood:** Glamorous, aspirational, machine-age optimism. The future as imagined by the Jazz Age.
- **Architectural elements:** Sunburst motifs, stepped crowns, geometric friezes, fluted columns, speed lines, stylized eagles/figures, terraced setbacks, grand lobbies with geometric floor patterns.
