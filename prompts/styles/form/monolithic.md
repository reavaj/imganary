# Monolithic

Single massive unbroken form dominating the space — 2001's black slab, brutalist concrete, Richard Serra's steel.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Presents a single, massive, unbroken form that dominates the visual field through sheer physical presence, geometric simplicity, and imposing scale.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - One dominant, massive, simple form
  - No surface detail or ornament
  - Overwhelming physical presence
  - Geometric purity (slab, cube, cylinder)
  - Viewer dwarfed by scale
- **Reference points:** 2001: A Space Odyssey (monolith), brutalist concrete architecture, Richard Serra (steel sculptures), Stonehenge, James Turrell (light rooms)
- **Pairs well with:** austere, monumental, stark, towering

## FLUX keywords
monolithic, massive block, solid form, imposing mass, brutalist, singular mass, towering solid, 2001 monolith, concrete slab, unbroken form
