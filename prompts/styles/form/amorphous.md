# Amorphous

Undefined, blobby, shapeless forms without clear edges — Hans Arp, lava lamps, smoke, amoeba, Tanguy's surrealism.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Dissolves hard edges and defined shapes into soft, flowing, undefined masses that suggest organic uncertainty and primordial formlessness.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - No defined edges or clear boundaries
  - Soft, blob-like, flowing masses
  - Organic but not structured (no stems, no skeletons)
  - Primordial, pre-form, embryonic quality
  - Floating, suspended, without gravity
- **Reference points:** Hans Arp (biomorphic sculpture), Yves Tanguy (surrealist landscapes), lava lamps, smoke photography, amoeba microscopy
- **Pairs well with:** ethereal, diffused, hallucinatory, translucent

## FLUX keywords
amorphous, shapeless, blobby, undefined form, soft blob, formless, organic blob, nebulous, cloud-like, flowing mass
