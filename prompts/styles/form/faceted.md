# Faceted

Surface broken into flat planes like a cut gemstone — Art Deco, low-poly 3D, Cubist sculpture, crystal formations.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Breaks smooth surfaces into discrete flat planes that each catch light differently, creating a crystalline, jewel-like, or digitally polygonal appearance.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Flat planes at varying angles to the light
  - Each facet catches light differently
  - Crystalline, gem-cut, prismatic quality
  - Sharp edges between planes
  - Angular segmentation of smooth forms
- **Reference points:** Art Deco decoration, low-poly 3D rendering, Cubist sculpture (Archipenko), crystal/mineral formations, diamond cutting
- **Pairs well with:** angular, geometric, jewel-toned, glossy

## FLUX keywords
faceted, crystal facets, low-poly, angular planes, cut gemstone, multi-faceted, prismatic surface, diamond cut, polygon faces, segmented
