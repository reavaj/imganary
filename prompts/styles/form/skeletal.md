# Skeletal

Structural bones exposed, framework visible, stripped to structure — wireframe 3D, Giger's biomechanics, X-ray photography.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Strips away surface and skin to reveal the underlying structural framework, making the architecture of form visible — bones, beams, ribs, wires.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Internal structure visible through or instead of surface
  - Bones, beams, ribs, wires, struts exposed
  - Transparency or absence of skin/cladding
  - Anatomical, engineering, or architectural logic
  - Both beautiful and unsettling — seeing what's normally hidden
- **Reference points:** H.R. Giger (Alien biomechanics), wireframe 3D rendering, X-ray photography, exposed-structure architecture (Centre Pompidou), anatomical illustration
- **Pairs well with:** angular, stark, eerie, monochromatic

## FLUX keywords
skeletal, wireframe, exposed structure, bare framework, bones visible, structural, anatomical, Giger biomechanical, X-ray, stripped to frame
