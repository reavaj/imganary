# Angular

Sharp diagonal and jagged geometry dominating the composition — Constructivism, Deconstructivist architecture, Futurist painting.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Replaces curves and soft forms with sharp intersections, aggressive diagonals, and pointed geometry, creating dynamic instability and visual aggression.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Pointed intersections and sharp corners
  - Aggressive diagonal lines dominating
  - No curves or soft transitions
  - Dynamic instability, visual tension
  - Fragmented, shattered, crystalline feeling
- **Reference points:** Russian Constructivism (El Lissitzky), Deconstructivist architecture (Gehry, Libeskind), Futurist painting (Boccioni), shattered glass, crystal formations
- **Pairs well with:** graphic, fragmented, expressionistic, faceted

## FLUX keywords
angular, sharp angles, diagonal, jagged, pointed, geometric sharp, aggressive lines, angular shapes, dynamic angles, edgy geometry
