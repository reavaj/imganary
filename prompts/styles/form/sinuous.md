# Sinuous

Flowing, snake-like S-curves creating rhythmic movement — Mucha's hair, Arabic calligraphy, Art Nouveau ironwork.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Introduces continuous, undulating S-curves that flow through the composition like ribbons or serpents, creating elegant, hypnotic visual rhythm.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Continuous S-curves and flowing lines
  - Serpentine, undulating movement
  - Elegant, rhythmic, dance-like quality
  - Lines that never stop — always continuing
  - Grace and fluidity as primary impression
- **Reference points:** Alphonse Mucha (poster art), Arabic calligraphy, Art Nouveau ironwork (Hector Guimard), Klimt's decorative patterns, ribbon sculpture
- **Pairs well with:** organic, ornate, opulent, intricate

## FLUX keywords
sinuous, S-curve, flowing lines, serpentine, undulating, Art Nouveau curves, elegant curves, ribbon-like, winding, fluid lines
