# Cracked

Surface broken into irregular fragments with visible fracture lines — dried earth, old oil paintings, craquelure, parched desert.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Fragments surfaces into irregular tessellated patterns through drying, aging, or stress, creating a map of time's passage and material fragility.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Irregular fracture lines across the surface
  - Tessellated fragments of varying sizes
  - Depth visible in the cracks themselves
  - Suggests age, drought, fragility, entropy
  - Pattern is organic, never perfectly regular
- **Reference points:** Craquelure on Old Master paintings, dried lake beds, parched earth, aged ceramic glaze, Anselm Kiefer
- **Pairs well with:** decayed, weathered, desolate, stark

## FLUX keywords
cracked, craquelure, dried earth cracks, fractured surface, broken texture, cracked paint, parched, fissured
