# Weathered

Surface showing signs of age, sun, wind, and rain — peeling paint, sun-faded posters, aged barn wood, Andrew Wyeth's America.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Applies the visible effects of prolonged environmental exposure to surfaces — cracking, peeling, fading, bleaching — creating a sense of history and endurance.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Peeling, flaking, cracking surface layers
  - Sun-bleached and rain-stained patterns
  - Organic, irregular degradation
  - Lived-in, storied, character-rich
  - Multiple generations of surface visible (paint over paint)
- **Reference points:** Andrew Wyeth (Christina's World), Walker Evans (Depression-era facades), aged barn wood, sun-faded billboards, patinated driftwood
- **Pairs well with:** nostalgic, decayed, desolate, bleached

## FLUX keywords
weathered, aged surface, worn, peeling paint, sun damaged, old wood, time-worn, faded, distressed surface
