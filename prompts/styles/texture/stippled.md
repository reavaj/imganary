# Stippled

Surface built from many small dots or points — Seurat's pointillism, engraving, Ben Day dots.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Constructs tone, value, and even color through accumulation of individual dots, creating a granular, optical-mixing texture visible on close inspection.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Individual dots visible at close range
  - Tone controlled by dot density, not dot size
  - Optical color mixing at viewing distance
  - Meticulous, labor-intensive quality
  - Scientific precision meets artistic patience
- **Reference points:** Georges Seurat (A Sunday on La Grande Jatte), stipple engraving, scientific illustration, tattoo shading
- **Pairs well with:** intricate, halftoned, graphic, minimal

stippled, pointillist, dot texture, stipple shading, tiny dots, point pattern, Seurat dots, dot rendering
