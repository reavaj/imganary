# Corroded

Metal eaten away by chemical reaction — rusted hulks, pitted surfaces, industrial decay, structural degradation.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Shows metal or material actively being consumed by oxidation, acid, or electrolysis, creating pitted, flaking, structurally compromised surfaces with distinctive orange-brown coloring.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Pitted, cratered surface from material loss
  - Orange-brown rust, white salt deposits, green verdigris
  - Flaking, crumbling edges
  - Structural weakness implied
  - Industrial, post-apocalyptic, abandoned
- **Reference points:** Anselm Kiefer (lead and iron works), shipwrecks, industrial ruins, Richard Serra weathering steel, post-industrial photography
- **Pairs well with:** decayed, oxidized, desolate, gritty

## FLUX keywords
corroded, rusted, rust, corrosion, pitted metal, oxidized iron, decaying metal, industrial rust, eaten away
