# Glossy

Smooth, reflective, polished surface quality — magazine covers, lacquered surfaces, Jeff Koons, vinyl records.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Gives surfaces a mirror-like, light-catching sheen that reflects the environment, creating a slick, commercial, and luxurious quality.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Specular highlights and reflections on surfaces
  - Mirror-like, wet-look finish
  - Commercial perfection, no flaws
  - Light bounces and slides across surfaces
  - High production value, polished aesthetic
- **Reference points:** Vogue covers, Jeff Koons balloon sculptures, automotive photography, vinyl record sleeves, patent leather
- **Pairs well with:** saturated, photorealistic, opulent, shallow-focus

## FLUX keywords
glossy, shiny, polished, reflective surface, lacquered, mirror finish, wet look, high gloss, slick surface
