# Impasto

Thick, raised paint applied with palette knife or heavy brush — Van Gogh's swirling skies, de Kooning's slabs of color.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Builds paint up into a three-dimensional surface that catches real light and casts real shadows, making the painting itself a sculptural object.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Paint sits above the surface, visibly thick
  - Palette knife marks and heavy brush ridges
  - Real shadows cast by paint thickness
  - Tactile, physical, sculptural quality
  - Energy and speed of application visible
- **Reference points:** Van Gogh (Starry Night), Willem de Kooning, Frank Auerbach, Nicolas de Staël, Lucian Freud
- **Pairs well with:** painterly, expressionistic, coarse, saturated

## FLUX keywords
impasto, thick paint, heavy brushstrokes, textured paint surface, palette knife, raised paint, sculptural brushwork, Van Gogh texture
