# Matte

Flat, non-reflective, uncoated finish — zine culture, brutalist concrete, uncoated paper stock.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Removes all sheen and reflectivity from surfaces, absorbing light rather than bouncing it, creating an understated, tactile, craft-oriented quality.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - No specular highlights or reflections
  - Light absorbed, not reflected
  - Tactile, touchable, craft feeling
  - Understated, anti-commercial
  - Chalky, dry surface quality
- **Reference points:** Zine printing, brutalist concrete, Bauhaus materials, uncoated stock, matte photo prints, matte ceramic
- **Pairs well with:** austere, minimal, muted, coarse

## FLUX keywords
matte finish, flat surface, non-reflective, uncoated, matte texture, dull finish, chalky surface, no sheen
