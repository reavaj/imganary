# Burnished

Polished by rubbing to a warm, smooth sheen — aged leather, polished bronze, worn wood handles, gold leaf.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Creates a warm, soft-focus reflectivity through friction and use over time, distinct from manufactured gloss — this sheen is earned, not applied.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Warm, soft metallic glow rather than mirror-sharp reflection
  - Smooth from repeated touch and use
  - Suggests age, care, craftsmanship
  - Golden-warm highlight quality
  - Depth of material visible beneath the sheen
- **Reference points:** Polished bronze sculpture, worn leather saddles, gold leaf on icons, aged brass door handles, Japanese urushi lacquer
- **Pairs well with:** opulent, jewel-toned, warm, ornate

## FLUX keywords
burnished, polished, warm sheen, golden polish, rubbed smooth, warm metallic glow, patina polish, aged shine
