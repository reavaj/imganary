# Translucent

Partially transparent, light passing through diffusely — frosted glass, wax, jellyfish, resin, alabaster.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Allows light to penetrate and scatter through materials without full transparency, creating a soft inner glow and mysterious depth within solid forms.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Light passes through but images don't resolve
  - Inner glow, subsurface luminosity
  - Soft, diffused edges where light enters
  - Ethereal, delicate, otherworldly
  - Depth visible within the material itself
- **Reference points:** Frosted glass architecture, wax sculpture, jellyfish photography, Dale Chihuly glass, alabaster lamps, resin art
- **Pairs well with:** ethereal, diffused, iridescent, soft-focus

## FLUX keywords
translucent, semi-transparent, frosted glass, light passing through, glowing, wax-like, backlit transparency, diffused transparency
