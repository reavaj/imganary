# Wide-angle

Barrel distortion, expanded space, dramatic perspective exaggeration — Terry Gilliam, fisheye, GoPro.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Stretches perspective through a short focal length, making close objects appear enormous while pushing distant objects further away, with characteristic barrel distortion at the edges.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Barrel distortion curving straight lines at edges
  - Exaggerated size difference between near and far
  - Rooms and spaces appear larger than life
  - Close objects distorted, sometimes comically
  - Dynamic, energetic, immersive feeling
- **Reference points:** Terry Gilliam (Brazil, Fear and Loathing), fisheye skateboard photography, GoPro action footage, real estate interiors, Jan Vermeer (wide-angle room views)
- **Pairs well with:** claustrophobic, towering, receding, expressionistic
