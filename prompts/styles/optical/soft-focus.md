# Soft-focus

Dreamy haze softening all edges, glamorous diffusion — Pictorialism, 1980s glamour, Vaseline-on-lens.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Wraps the entire image in a gentle, luminous haze where highlights bleed into shadows and all edges soften, creating an idealized, romantic, dreamlike quality.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - All edges softened, nothing truly sharp
  - Highlights bloom and bleed into surrounding areas
  - Flattering, forgiving, idealized rendering
  - Romantic, dreamlike, nostalgic atmosphere
  - Gauzy, veiled, looking-through-fabric quality
- **Reference points:** Pictorialism movement (early 1900s), 1980s glamour photography, Douglas Kirkland (Marilyn Monroe), Vaseline-on-lens technique, David Hamilton
- **Pairs well with:** ethereal, pastel, nostalgic, diffused

## FLUX keywords
soft focus, dreamy, hazy, diffused, Vaseline lens, soft glow, romantic soft, glamour photography, glowing edges, pictorialist, gauzy
