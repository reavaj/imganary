# Telephoto

Compressed depth, flattened planes, distant observation — Hitchcock's Vertigo, surveillance photography, sports.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Compresses the depth of a scene by using a long focal length, stacking distant planes on top of each other and flattening spatial relationships.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Depth planes stacked and compressed
  - Flat, poster-like spatial relationships
  - Voyeuristic, distant observation feel
  - Background appears unnaturally close to subject
  - Heat haze and atmospheric distortion visible
- **Reference points:** Hitchcock (Vertigo dolly zoom, Rear Window), surveillance photography, sports telephoto, Akira Kurosawa (telephoto crowd scenes)
- **Pairs well with:** layered, stark, frozen, static

## FLUX keywords
telephoto, compressed perspective, long lens, flat depth, 200mm, stacked layers, distant observation, compressed space, surveillance, telephoto compression
