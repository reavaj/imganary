# Anamorphic

Oval bokeh, horizontal lens flares, cinematic widescreen distortion — Panavision glass, J.J. Abrams, Roger Deakins.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Introduces the signature optical artifacts of anamorphic cinema lenses — horizontally stretched bokeh, blue-amber flares, and subtle barrel distortion that screams "movie."
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Oval/elliptical bokeh (not round)
  - Horizontal lens flares (blue, amber, or white)
  - Subtle barrel distortion at frame edges
  - 2.39:1 widescreen framing
  - Unmistakable "cinematic" quality
- **Reference points:** Panavision C-series and G-series lenses, J.J. Abrams (Star Trek flares), Roger Deakins, Michael Mann (Heat, Collateral)
- **Pairs well with:** panoramic, neon, volumetric, shallow-focus
