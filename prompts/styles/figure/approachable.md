# Approachable

Natural, relatable, warm presence — the face in a crowd you'd trust, everyday beauty without artifice.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Renders human subjects with believable, moderate attractiveness — natural skin, relaxed posture, genuine expression — the kind of face that feels familiar rather than extraordinary.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Slight facial asymmetry, softer bone structure, rounded features
  - Natural skin with minor imperfections — faint freckles, subtle unevenness, visible but not prominent pores
  - Average eye spacing, warm and open expression, natural lashes
  - Hair with natural body, casual styling, some flyaways or loose strands
  - Average or slighly overweight build, natural posture, relaxed and unselfconscious frame
  - Lips of normal proportion, not overly defined
  - Genuine smile or easy resting expression, unguarded and inviting
  - Nose with natural character — slightly wide, gently bumped, or softly rounded
- **Reference points:** Dorothea Lange portraits, Dutch Golden Age genre painting (Vermeer, Hals), street photography, indie film casting
- **Pairs well with:** intimate, nostalgic, diffused, matte

## FLUX keywords
natural features, approachable face, warm expression, genuine smile, relaxed posture, everyday beauty, slight imperfections, soft features, unposed, natural skin texture
