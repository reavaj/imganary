# Approachable

Natural, relatable, warm presence — the face in a crowd you'd trust, everyday beauty without artifice.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Renders human subjects with believable, moderate attractiveness — natural skin, relaxed posture, genuine expression — the kind of face that feels familiar rather than extraordinary.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Slight facial asymmetry, softer bone structure, rounded features
  - oval, round or square face, slightly puffy cheeks and eyes
  - Natural skin with minor imperfections — faint freckles, subtle unevenness, visible but not prominent pores
  - Average eye spacing, open expression, natural lashes
  - no makeup unless otherwise specified
  - Hair with natural body and casual styling — could be tied back, tucked under a hat, loosely parted, air-dried, or just not fussed over
  - Varied body types — could be stocky, short, tall and lanky, pear-shaped, curvy, athletic, soft and heavy, wiry, or just average. Natural posture, relaxed and unselfconscious frame
  - Lips are thin without lipstick
  - Genuine smile or easy resting expression, unguarded and inviting
  - Nose with natural character — slightly wide, button shaped, gently bumped, softly rounded, or slightly large
- **Reference points:** street photography, indie film casting
- **Pairs well with:** intimate, nostalgic, diffused, matte

## FLUX keywords
natural features, approachable face, round or oval face shape, soft rounded jawline, slightly puffy cheeks, warm expression, genuine smile, realistic body type, relaxed posture, everyday beauty, slight imperfections, soft features, unposed, natural skin texture
