# Characterful

Lived-in, distinctive, unforgettable faces — Steve McCurry portraits, Lucian Freud nudes, Dorothea Lange's migrants.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Renders human subjects with pronounced, unconventional features that tell a story — deep lines, weathered skin, unusual proportions — prioritizing presence and authenticity over conventional beauty.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Pronounced facial asymmetry, unusual proportions, exaggerated features
  - Heavily textured skin — visible pores, deep creases, sun damage, scarring, age spots
  - Deep-set or close-set eyes, heavy lids, crow's feet, bloodshot sclera
  - Thinning, wiry, or unkempt hair — receding hairline, coarse texture, grey streaks
  - Stocky or gaunt build, rounded shoulders, uneven posture, thick or bony hands
  - Thin or uneven lips, chapped texture
  - Weathered intensity in expression — furrowed brow, guarded gaze, deep nasolabial folds
  - Large, crooked, or bulbous nose with visible pores and broken capillaries
- **Reference points:** Steve McCurry (Afghan Girl), Lucian Freud (Benefits Supervisor Sleeping), Diane Arbus, August Sander typologies, Sebastiao Salgado workers
- **Pairs well with:** gritty, harsh, chiaroscuro, weathered

## FLUX keywords
weathered face, deep wrinkles, rough skin, prominent nose, sunken eyes, heavy brow, lined features, raw portrait, lived-in face, coarse texture, unidealized, rugged features
