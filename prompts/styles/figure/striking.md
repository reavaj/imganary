# Striking

Arresting, symmetrical, magnetic beauty — supermodel editorials, classical sculpture, golden-ratio faces.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Renders human subjects with the idealized physical attributes associated with high conventional attractiveness — symmetry, proportion, luminous skin, and effortless poise.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - High facial symmetry, strong cheekbones, defined jawline
  - Flawless, luminous, even-toned skin with no visible pores
  - Large, widely spaced eyes with vivid iris color and full lashes
  - Thick, lustrous hair with volume and perfectly groomed texture
  - Lean or athletic build, elongated neck, defined collarbones
  - Full, well-defined lips with natural color contrast
  - Poised, confident expression — magnetic gaze, effortless composure
  - Straight, proportionate nose with refined bridge
- **Reference points:** Renaissance ideals (Botticelli's Venus), golden-ratio proportions, high-fashion editorial photography, classical Greek sculpture
- **Pairs well with:** ethereal, opulent, monumental, glossy

## FLUX keywords
striking beauty, high cheekbones, symmetrical face, defined jawline, luminous skin, piercing eyes, sculpted features, flawless complexion, angular bone structure, magnetic presence
