# Desolate

Empty, windswept, abandoned, post-human emptiness — Mad Max wastelands, Andrew Wyeth's fields, Cormac McCarthy's road.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Empties the landscape of life and comfort, leaving only vast, indifferent terrain that suggests abandonment, extinction, or hostile nature.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Vast empty landscapes with minimal features
  - Ruins, abandoned structures, dried vegetation
  - Overcast or bleached-white sky
  - Wind-whipped, dust-blown, eroded surfaces
  - Human absence or tiny isolated figures
- **Reference points:** Mad Max (Fury Road), Andrew Wyeth (Christina's World), The Road (2009), Chernobyl exclusion zone, Dust Bowl photography
- **Pairs well with:** bleached, desaturated, harsh, panoramic

## FLUX keywords
desolate, barren, empty, wasteland, abandoned, bleak, windswept, post-apocalyptic, forsaken, lonely landscape
