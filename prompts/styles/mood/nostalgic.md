# Nostalgic

Longing for the past, memory-colored, sentimental warmth — Super 8 home movies, faded Polaroids, Kodachrome summers.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Applies the visual artifacts of aged media — warm casts, soft edges, grain, light leaks — to create a sense of looking back at a treasured, unreachable past.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Warm amber/yellow color cast
  - Soft, slightly out-of-focus edges
  - Film grain, light leaks, vignetting
  - Analog artifacts (scratches, dust, sprocket marks)
  - Sentimental, tender, bittersweet feeling
- **Reference points:** Super 8 home movies, Polaroid photography, Kodachrome slides, Stranger Things, Wes Anderson period films
- **Pairs well with:** grainy, tungsten, bleached, soft-focus

## FLUX keywords
nostalgic, vintage, retro, old photograph, faded memory, warm tones, Polaroid, film photograph, sepia tinted, sentimental
