# Monumental

Awe through overwhelming scale, human dwarfed by structure — Soviet posters, Albert Speer's columns, IMAX landscapes.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Uses extreme scale difference between environment and human figures to create a sense of awe, power, or insignificance through sheer physical dominance.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Massive structures or landscapes dominating the frame
  - Tiny human figures for scale reference
  - Low camera angle amplifying height
  - Dramatic sky as backdrop
  - Symmetry reinforcing power and intention
- **Reference points:** Leni Riefenstahl, Soviet constructivist posters, Albert Speer architecture, 2001: A Space Odyssey, IMAX nature films, Egyptian temples
- **Pairs well with:** towering, symmetrical, stark, vast

## FLUX keywords
monumental, massive, grand scale, towering, imposing, epic, colossal, awe-inspiring, dwarfing, heroic scale
