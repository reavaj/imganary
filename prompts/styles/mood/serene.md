# Serene

Deep calm, peaceful contemplation, visual quietude — Ozu's tatami rooms, Monet's water lilies, Sugimoto's seascapes.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Empties the image of tension, creating a meditative calm through horizontal composition, soft light, and minimal visual disturbance.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Dominant horizontal lines (horizons, water surfaces)
  - Soft, even, diffused light
  - Muted or pastel color palette
  - Still water, empty sky, open fields
  - No visual conflict or tension
- **Reference points:** Yasujirō Ozu, Monet (Water Lilies), Hiroshi Sugimoto (Seascapes), Mark Rothko, Japanese zen gardens
- **Pairs well with:** diffused, minimal, pastel, muted

## FLUX keywords
serene, peaceful, calm, tranquil, quiet, still, contemplative, zen, meditative, gentle atmosphere
