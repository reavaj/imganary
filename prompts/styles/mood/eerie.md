# Eerie

Uncanny, subtly wrong, unsettling without explicit horror — Lynch's suburbia, Beksiński's landscapes, liminal spaces.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Introduces a subtle wrongness into familiar scenes — slightly off colors, unnatural stillness, empty spaces that should be occupied — creating dread through the uncanny.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Familiar scenes made subtly wrong
  - Unnatural stillness or silence implied
  - Empty spaces that feel watched or occupied
  - Slightly shifted colors (too green, too yellow)
  - No explicit threat visible, but tension pervasive
- **Reference points:** David Lynch (Twin Peaks, Blue Velvet), Zdzisław Beksiński, Gregory Crewdson, liminal space photography, Edward Gorey
- **Pairs well with:** fluorescent, desaturated, soft-focus, static

## FLUX keywords
eerie, uncanny, unsettling, strange, creepy, unnatural, disturbing calm, liminal, wrong, otherworldly dread
