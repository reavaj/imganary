# Melancholic

Quiet sadness, beautiful loneliness, pensive stillness — Bergman's silences, Hopper's empty diners, Tarkovsky's rain.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Infuses the image with a gentle, pervasive sadness through muted color, solitary subjects, and empty spaces that amplify emotional weight.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Muted, drained color palette
  - Solitary figures in empty environments
  - Twilight, overcast, or rainy conditions
  - Still, quiet, contemplative compositions
  - Beauty inseparable from sadness
- **Reference points:** Ingmar Bergman, Edward Hopper (Nighthawks), Andrei Tarkovsky, Gregory Crewdson, Wong Kar-wai (In the Mood for Love)
- **Pairs well with:** desaturated, muted, diffused, nostalgic

## FLUX keywords
melancholic, sad, lonely, pensive, quiet sadness, solitary, wistful, somber mood, contemplative, twilight mood
