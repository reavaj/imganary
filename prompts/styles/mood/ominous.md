# Ominous

Building dread, something terrible approaching — Kubrick's corridors, storm fronts, Lynch's uncanny suburbia.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Creates visual tension and foreboding through unnatural stillness, oppressive composition, and lighting that suggests threat without revealing its source.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Dark, heavy skies or ceilings pressing down
  - Unnatural stillness before violence
  - Distorted or exaggerated perspective
  - Shadows concealing rather than revealing
  - Color drained or shifted toward sickly tones
- **Reference points:** Kubrick (The Shining hallways), David Lynch (Blue Velvet), Hitchcock, storm photography, Caspar David Friedrich
- **Pairs well with:** underexposed, volumetric, desaturated, receding

## FLUX keywords
ominous, foreboding, threatening, dark atmosphere, impending doom, sinister, menacing, dread, looming danger, dark clouds
