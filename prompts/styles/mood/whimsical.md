# Whimsical

Playful, lighthearted, imaginative, charmingly surreal — Miyazaki's worlds, Magritte's puzzles, Wes Anderson's dollhouses.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Introduces gentle impossibility and childlike wonder, bending reality with a light touch rather than breaking it, creating charm and delight.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Impossible physics played straight (floating, flying)
  - Candy-colored or pastel palette
  - Gentle humor, no menace
  - Storybook or fairy-tale logic
  - Miniature, dollhouse-like precision
- **Reference points:** Hayao Miyazaki (Totoro, Spirited Away), René Magritte, Wes Anderson, children's book illustration, Michel Gondry
- **Pairs well with:** pastel, organic, cel-shaded, stylized

## FLUX keywords
whimsical, playful, imaginative, fantastical, charming, lighthearted, magical, storybook, fairy tale, wonder
