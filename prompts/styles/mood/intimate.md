# Intimate

Close, personal, private moment observed — Cassavetes' handheld truth, Nan Goldin's bedrooms, Sally Mann's family.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Brings the viewer uncomfortably, beautifully close to the subject, creating a sense of private access, vulnerability, and emotional proximity.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Close framing, shallow depth of field
  - Warm tones, soft available light
  - Eye contact or unguarded expressions
  - Private spaces (bedrooms, kitchens, bathrooms)
  - Vulnerability visible, no performance
- **Reference points:** John Cassavetes, Nan Goldin (The Ballad of Sexual Dependency), Sally Mann, Richard Linklater, Claire Denis
- **Pairs well with:** candlelit, shallow-focus, tungsten, grainy

## FLUX keywords
intimate, close-up, personal, private, tender, warm, vulnerable, gentle, soft focus portrait, quiet moment
