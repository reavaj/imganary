# Claustrophobic

Tight, enclosed, no escape, walls closing in — Das Boot, Alien's air vents, submarine corridors.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Constricts the visual space around the subject using tight framing, low ceilings, narrow corridors, and close walls, creating a visceral sense of entrapment.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Tight framing with no breathing room
  - Low ceilings, narrow walls visible in frame
  - Wide-angle distortion amplifying confinement
  - Shallow depth compressing space further
  - Subjects physically pressed by environment
- **Reference points:** Das Boot, Alien (vent crawl sequences), Buried (2010), submarine films, elevator scenes, MRI machines
- **Pairs well with:** harsh, fluorescent, underexposed, wide-angle

## FLUX keywords
claustrophobic, tight space, enclosed, cramped, narrow, confined, suffocating, close walls, no escape, trapped
