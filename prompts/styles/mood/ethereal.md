# Ethereal

Dreamlike, otherworldly, impossibly delicate beauty — Pre-Raphaelites, Tarkovsky's visions, Tim Walker's fashion fantasies.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Dissolves the boundary between real and unreal, wrapping subjects in soft light and haze to create a floating, heavenly, just-out-of-reach quality.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Soft, glowing, diffused light everywhere
  - Floating, weightless quality to subjects
  - Pale, luminous color palette
  - Hazy edges, nothing razor-sharp
  - Otherworldly, angelic, transcendent mood
- **Reference points:** Pre-Raphaelites (Waterhouse, Rossetti), Tarkovsky (Solaris, Stalker), Tim Walker fashion, Gregory Crewdson twilight
- **Pairs well with:** diffused, overexposed, soft-focus, translucent

## FLUX keywords
ethereal, dreamlike, otherworldly, heavenly, soft glow, floating, angelic, mystical light, delicate, gossamer
