# Austere

Stripped to essentials, severe, disciplined restraint — Bresson's minimalism, Bauhaus purity, Shaker furniture.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Removes all decoration, ornament, and excess, leaving only what is structurally or functionally necessary, creating an atmosphere of severe discipline.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - No decoration, no ornament, no excess
  - Bare materials: raw wood, plain concrete, white walls
  - Geometric precision, clean lines
  - Monastic, disciplined, puritanical
  - Beauty through reduction, not addition
- **Reference points:** Robert Bresson, Bauhaus (Gropius, Mies van der Rohe), Shaker furniture, Tadao Ando architecture, monastic interiors
- **Pairs well with:** matte, minimal, stark, geometric

## FLUX keywords
austere, severe, minimal, stripped, bare, sparse, disciplined, monastic, unadorned, restrained
