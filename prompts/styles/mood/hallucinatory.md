# Hallucinatory

Reality warped, psychedelic distortion, fever-dream visuals — Jodorowsky's visions, psychedelic posters, Enter the Void.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Breaks the rules of optical reality — impossible colors, melting forms, fractal repetition — creating visuals that simulate altered states of consciousness.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Colors that don't exist in nature
  - Melting, warping, breathing forms
  - Fractal patterns and kaleidoscopic repetition
  - Sensory overload, visual noise
  - Reality recognizable but fundamentally wrong
- **Reference points:** Alejandro Jodorowsky (The Holy Mountain), psychedelic concert posters, Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas, Enter the Void (Gaspar Noé), Alex Grey
- **Pairs well with:** neon, chromatic, saturated, expressionistic

## FLUX keywords
hallucinatory, psychedelic, trippy, surreal, distorted reality, fever dream, acid trip, kaleidoscopic, mind-bending, visionary
