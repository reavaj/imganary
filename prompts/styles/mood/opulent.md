# Opulent

Excess, luxury, visual richness overwhelming the eye — Baz Luhrmann's parties, Versailles, Klimt's gold, Mucha's jewels.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Fills every surface with richness, ornamentation, and material luxury, creating a visual experience of excess that borders on overwhelming.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Gold, velvet, silk, jewels everywhere
  - Every surface decorated, nothing plain
  - Warm, rich, saturated color palette
  - Abundance overflowing the frame
  - Sensory overload through material wealth
- **Reference points:** Baz Luhrmann (The Great Gatsby), Versailles interiors, Klimt (The Kiss), Mucha posters, Bollywood set design
- **Pairs well with:** saturated, jewel-toned, ornate, burnished

## FLUX keywords
opulent, gold leaf, silk damask, cut crystal chandelier, velvet upholstery, marble columns, Versailles interior, gilded ornament, heavy brocade, overflowing abundance
