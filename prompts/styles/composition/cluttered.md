# Cluttered

Dense, packed, overwhelming visual information — Terry Gilliam's chaos, Bosch's hellscapes, horror vacui.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Fills every square inch of the frame with visual information, leaving no rest for the eye and creating a sense of abundance, chaos, or overwhelming density.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - No negative space — everything filled
  - Horror vacui (fear of empty space)
  - Eye has no clear path, wanders constantly
  - Maximalist, more-is-more philosophy
  - Can feel abundant (markets) or suffocating (hoarding)
- **Reference points:** Hieronymus Bosch (Garden of Earthly Delights), Terry Gilliam (Brazil, 12 Monkeys), Where's Waldo, cabinet of curiosities, Tokyo streets
- **Pairs well with:** ornate, intricate, hallucinatory, saturated

## FLUX keywords
cluttered, dense, packed, maximalist, horror vacui, overflowing, crowded, busy, filled to the brim, visual overload
