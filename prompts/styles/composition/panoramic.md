# Panoramic

Ultra-wide horizontal format capturing vast expanses — Leone's westerns, CinemaScope, Chinese scroll painting.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Stretches the frame to an extreme horizontal ratio (2.39:1 or wider), emphasizing landscape, horizon, and environmental sweep over individual subjects.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Ultra-wide aspect ratio (2.39:1 or wider)
  - Horizon dominates the composition
  - Environment as primary subject
  - Epic, sweeping, environmental storytelling
  - Human figures small within vast space
- **Reference points:** Sergio Leone (Once Upon a Time in the West), David Lean (Lawrence of Arabia), CinemaScope, Chinese scroll painting, Ansel Adams panoramas
- **Pairs well with:** vast, desolate, serene, deep-focus

## FLUX keywords
panoramic, ultra-wide, widescreen, CinemaScope, vast landscape, wide horizon, sweeping vista, epic wide shot, expansive view
