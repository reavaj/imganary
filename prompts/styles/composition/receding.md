# Receding

Deep one-point perspective drawing the eye to a vanishing point — Kubrick's corridors, Renaissance perspective, train tracks.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Establishes a single vanishing point that pulls the viewer's eye deep into the image along converging lines, creating hypnotic depth and directional force.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Strong one-point perspective with clear vanishing point
  - Parallel lines converging toward center
  - Deep spatial illusion on flat surface
  - Hypnotic, tunnel-like pull into the image
  - Architectural or environmental subjects suit this best
- **Reference points:** Kubrick (The Shining hallway, 2001 corridors), Renaissance perspective (Brunelleschi), train track photography, highway photography
- **Pairs well with:** symmetrical, claustrophobic, monumental, towering

## FLUX keywords
receding perspective, one-point perspective, vanishing point, converging lines, deep corridor, infinite depth, tunnel vision, disappearing into distance
