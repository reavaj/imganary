# Candlelit

Warm, flickering, intimate single-flame illumination — Barry Lyndon's natural light, Dutch Golden Age interiors, medieval chambers.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Limits illumination to the warm, low-intensity radius of candle flames, creating deep fall-off, rich shadows, and an intimate period atmosphere.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Deep orange-gold color temperature
  - Rapid light fall-off beyond the source
  - Flickering, unstable quality
  - Intimate, small radius of visibility
  - Period-authentic, pre-electric feeling
- **Reference points:** Kubrick (Barry Lyndon, shot at f/0.7), Vermeer, Georges de La Tour, medieval banquet scenes
- **Pairs well with:** intimate, painterly, nostalgic, chiaroscuro
