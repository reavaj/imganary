# Silhouetted

Subject reduced to a pure dark shape against a bright background — Saul Bass title design, shadow puppetry, the sunset figure.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Eliminates all surface detail from the subject, reducing them to a flat, black contour defined entirely by their outline against a luminous background.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Subject is pure black with no interior detail
  - Background is bright and dominant
  - Shape and contour carry all meaning
  - Highly graphic, poster-like read
  - Strong emotional impact through simplification
- **Reference points:** Saul Bass (Vertigo, Anatomy of a Murder), shadow puppetry, sunset photography, propaganda posters
- **Pairs well with:** backlit, graphic, stark, monumental

## FLUX keywords
silhouette, silhouetted figure, dark outline, black shape against light, shadow figure, backlit silhouette, stark outline, high contrast shape
