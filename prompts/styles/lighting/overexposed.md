# Overexposed

Blown-out highlights and washed-out brightness creating an ethereal, bleached quality — the dreamy excess of light in Malick and Lomo photography.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Floods the image with more light than the medium can capture, dissolving detail in the highlights into pure white and creating a hazy, transcendent glow.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Lost detail in whites and bright areas
  - Ethereal, heavenly glow bleeding from light sources
  - Reduced contrast in highlights, preserved shadows
  - Bleached, faded color in bright zones
  - Dream-sequence, memory-like quality
- **Reference points:** Terrence Malick (The Tree of Life), Lomo photography, Sofia Coppola, dream sequences, light-leak Polaroids
- **Pairs well with:** ethereal, bleached, soft-focus, pastel

## FLUX keywords
overexposed, blown out highlights, bright, washed out, high key, dreamy light, white glow, overlit
