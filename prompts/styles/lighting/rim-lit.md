# Rim-lit

Edge lighting that traces the subject's contour with a bright outline, separating them from the background — portrait photography's secret weapon.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Places a light behind and to the side of the subject, creating a bright edge that defines their silhouette and lifts them off the background.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Thin bright line tracing the subject's outline
  - Dark front with bright edges
  - Strong subject-background separation
  - Hair and shoulders glow
  - Often combined with softer front fill
- **Reference points:** Portrait photography, sci-fi cinematography, concert photography, Vermeer's edge light
- **Pairs well with:** backlit, chiaroscuro, silhouetted, dramatic

## FLUX keywords
rim lighting, edge light, outline glow, backlight edge, bright rim, contour lighting, subject separation, glowing outline
