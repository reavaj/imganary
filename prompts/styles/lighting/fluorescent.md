# Fluorescent

Cool, green-tinged, flat institutional overhead light — the Nostromo corridor, the hospital ward, the office at 2am.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Casts a sickly green-white pall from overhead tube fixtures, creating flat, unflattering illumination associated with institutional and industrial spaces.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Green-cyan color cast on skin and walls
  - Flat, shadowless overhead illumination
  - Unflattering, clinical, institutional feel
  - Buzzing, sterile, inhuman quality
  - Dark eye sockets from overhead angle
- **Reference points:** Alien (Nostromo interiors), hospital dramas, office photography, David Fincher (Zodiac), convenience store photography
- **Pairs well with:** eerie, claustrophobic, stark, desaturated

## FLUX keywords
fluorescent lighting, green tint, office lighting, institutional light, overhead tubes, cold green light, hospital lighting, harsh overhead
