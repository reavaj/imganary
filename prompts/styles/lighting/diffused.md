# Diffused

Soft, even, shadowless illumination where light wraps around forms — Vermeer's window light, the overcast day, the softbox.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Scatters light evenly across the scene, eliminating hard shadows and creating gentle tonal gradients that flatter surfaces and reduce contrast.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - No hard-edged shadows anywhere in the frame
  - Gentle, gradual tonal transitions
  - Flattering, even illumination on skin
  - Low contrast, soft feeling
  - Light seems to come from everywhere at once
- **Reference points:** Vermeer (Girl with a Pearl Earring), overcast day photography, softbox portraiture, cloudy-bright natural light
- **Pairs well with:** serene, pastel, intimate, ethereal

## FLUX keywords
diffused light, soft lighting, overcast, even illumination, no harsh shadows, soft glow, gentle light, cloudy day lighting
