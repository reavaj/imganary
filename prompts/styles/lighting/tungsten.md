# Tungsten

Warm amber-orange glow of incandescent bulbs — the cozy interior, the late-night diner, Wong Kar-wai's golden rooms.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Bathes the scene in warm orange-amber light from practical incandescent sources, creating an intimate, nostalgic, lived-in warmth.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Orange-amber color cast throughout
  - Warm skin tones, golden highlights
  - Cozy, interior, nighttime feeling
  - Visible practical light sources (lamps, bulbs)
  - Cool blue window light as contrast
- **Reference points:** Kubrick (Barry Lyndon, Eyes Wide Shut), Wong Kar-wai, Edward Hopper diners, late-night café scenes
- **Pairs well with:** nostalgic, intimate, candlelit, shallow-focus

## FLUX keywords
tungsten lighting, warm interior light, amber glow, incandescent, warm orange light, cozy lighting, yellow-orange light, warm practical lights
