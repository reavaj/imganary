# 3D Render / CGI

Clean, modern 3D rendering — the aesthetic of product visualization, motion graphics, and contemporary digital art. Octane, Blender, Cinema 4D look.

## Visual DNA
- **Lighting:** Three-point studio HDRI setups. Soft area lights with crisp shadows. Global illumination with subtle color bounce. Caustics through glass and liquid.
- **Composition:** Floating objects in infinite void (studio backdrop). Isometric or slight perspective. Clean, minimal staging. Single hero object or small arrangements.
- **Color palette:** Monochromatic pastels (blush pink, clay beige, soft grey) or bold saturated primaries. Gradient backgrounds. Subsurface scattering in translucent materials.
- **Textures & materials:** Smooth matte clay, glossy plastic, frosted glass, brushed metal, liquid chrome. Perfect surfaces — no imperfections. Soft rubber, inflated forms, bubble-like shapes.
- **Mood:** Satisfying, tactile, premium. Objects you want to touch. Clean and slightly surreal.
- **Rendering:** Path-traced with subtle depth of field. Soft ambient occlusion in crevices. Physically accurate reflections and refractions. No noise, no grain — pristine.

## FLUX keywords
3D render, CGI, Octane render, Blender, Cinema 4D, studio lighting, isometric, product visualization, glossy, matte, clay render
