# Cyberpunk

The neon-drenched, rain-soaked future-noir of Blade Runner, Ghost in the Shell, and Akira. High tech, low life.

## Visual DNA
- **Lighting:** Neon signage as primary light source — pink, cyan, purple. Volumetric fog and rain catching light beams. Reflections on every wet surface. Dark environments punctuated by intense color.
- **Composition:** Dense, layered, claustrophobic. Vertical cityscapes receding upward. Foreground clutter — cables, pipes, signage. Deep perspective down narrow alleys.
- **Color palette:** Dark base (near-black) with neon accents — hot pink, electric blue, toxic green. Warm tungsten interiors against cold blue exteriors. Orange sodium vapor streetlights.
- **Textures & materials:** Wet concrete, corroded metal, holographic displays, exposed wiring. Chrome and rust coexisting. Translucent plastic, LED strips, rain-slicked asphalt.
- **Mood:** Alienated, seductive, overwhelming. Beauty in decay. Technology as both salvation and prison.
- **Camera/rendering:** Anamorphic lens flares, chromatic aberration. Shallow depth of field with neon bokeh. Film grain. Slightly desaturated except for neon sources.
