# Vaporwave

The nostalgic, ironic digital aesthetic of early internet culture — Windows 95, dead malls, Greek statues, and consumer capitalism as fever dream.

## Visual DNA
- **Lighting:** Soft, diffused, artificial. Pink and purple ambient glow. Sunset gradients as backgrounds. Everything slightly overexposed and dreamy.
- **Composition:** Centered, symmetrical, deliberately "bad" — like clip art or a PowerPoint template. Floating objects in empty space. Tiled floors receding to infinity (checkerboard perspective grids).
- **Color palette:** Pastel pink, lavender, mint green, baby blue. Hot magenta accents. Sunset gradients (pink → orange → purple). Chrome and iridescent surfaces.
- **Textures & materials:** Smooth chrome, polished marble (Greek busts), CRT scan lines, pixelated edges, translucent plastic. Palm trees, dolphins, Roman columns — all decontextualized.
- **Mood:** Nostalgic, melancholic, absurdist. Consumerism as a beautiful, empty religion. Serenity through artificiality.
- **Rendering:** Clean digital surfaces mixed with deliberately low-res artifacts. VHS tracking lines, JPEG compression, glitch effects. 3D-rendered objects with early CGI quality (smooth, plastic).
