# Studio Product Photography

Clean, precise commercial photography designed to make objects look desirable. The Apple keynote / luxury brand catalog aesthetic.

## Visual DNA
- **Lighting:** Perfectly controlled softboxes, gradient backgrounds. Specular highlights on edges. No visible light source — just flawless illumination.
- **Composition:** Object centered or slightly off-center with generous negative space. Hero angle — slightly elevated, looking down at 15-30 degrees.
- **Color palette:** Monochromatic backgrounds (white, black, deep navy). Object colors pop against neutral. Occasional colored gel accent.
- **Textures & materials:** Brushed aluminum, matte ceramic, polished glass, soft-touch rubber. Every surface texture rendered with obsessive clarity.
- **Mood:** Premium, aspirational, clinical precision. The object is the star.
- **Camera:** Medium format, f/8-f/11 for maximum sharpness. Focus stacked. Zero grain, zero aberration.
