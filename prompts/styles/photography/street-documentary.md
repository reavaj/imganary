# Street / Documentary Photography

Candid, unposed moments captured in urban environments. The aesthetic of photojournalism and street photography — raw, immediate, human.

## Visual DNA
- **Lighting:** Available light only — harsh midday sun, neon signage at night, overcast flat light. Never looks "lit."
- **Composition:** Imperfect framing, slightly tilted horizons, layered foreground/background action. Decisive moment timing.
- **Color palette:** Muted, slightly desaturated. Warm tungsten cast at night, cool blue in shadow. Or classic black and white with rich tonal range.
- **Textures & materials:** Concrete, wet asphalt, peeling paint, worn denim, weathered faces. Urban grit.
- **Mood:** Intimate, voyeuristic, melancholic or quietly joyful. A sense of stolen moments.
- **Camera:** Shot on 35mm film (Tri-X grain, Portra color). Motion blur acceptable. Slight vignetting from fast wide-angle lenses.

## FLUX keywords
street photography, 35mm film, candid, documentary, Kodak Tri-X, grainy, available light, urban
