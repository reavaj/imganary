# Editorial Fashion Photography

High-end fashion editorial shot for a glossy magazine spread. Clean, controlled studio or dramatic on-location setting.

## Visual DNA
- **Lighting:** Harsh studio strobes with sharp shadows, or diffused natural window light. Rim lighting to separate subject from background. High contrast.
- **Composition:** Strong geometric framing, negative space used deliberately. Rule of thirds or dead-center symmetry. Full-body or three-quarter crop.
- **Color palette:** Desaturated neutrals with one accent color, or rich saturated monochromatic schemes. Skin tones slightly cooled.
- **Textures & materials:** Silk, leather, glossy vinyl, sheer fabrics. Hard reflective surfaces — marble, glass, chrome.
- **Mood:** Aloof, aspirational, slightly confrontational. Subjects never smile — fierce or contemplative.
- **Camera:** Shot on medium format digital (Phase One / Hasselblad look). Shallow depth of field on close-ups, deep focus on full-body. Razor-sharp detail.

## FLUX keywords
editorial photography, high fashion, Vogue, studio lighting, medium format, shallow depth of field, haute couture
