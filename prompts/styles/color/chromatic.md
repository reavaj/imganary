# Chromatic

Full rainbow spectrum visible, prismatic light dispersion — Pink Floyd's prism, stained glass, spectral refraction.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Introduces the full visible spectrum into the image, either through light refraction, prismatic effects, or deliberate rainbow color inclusion, creating a sense of optical wonder.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - All colors of the spectrum present simultaneously
  - Light splitting into component wavelengths
  - Prismatic edges and rainbow bands
  - Scientific beauty, physics made visible
  - Often associated with glass, water, crystal
- **Reference points:** Pink Floyd (Dark Side of the Moon prism), stained glass windows, crystal chandeliers, chromatic aberration in lenses, rainbow refraction
- **Pairs well with:** iridescent, ethereal, hallucinatory, translucent

## FLUX keywords
chromatic, prismatic, rainbow, spectrum, light refraction, color dispersion, full spectrum, prism effect, spectral colors
