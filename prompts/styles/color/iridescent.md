# Iridescent

Color-shifting, rainbow-on-surface effect that changes with viewing angle — oil slicks, soap bubbles, holographic foil, abalone shell.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Applies a prismatic, angle-dependent color shift to surfaces, creating rainbow gradients that shimmer and change, suggesting both futurism and natural optics.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Colors shift and flow across surfaces
  - Rainbow gradients without distinct boundaries
  - Pearlescent, opalescent sheen
  - Futuristic or organic depending on context
  - Light-reactive, dynamic, alive
- **Reference points:** Oil slick photography, holographic foil packaging, abalone shell, soap bubbles, album cover art (Björk), fashion textiles
- **Pairs well with:** ethereal, glossy, chromatic, translucent

## FLUX keywords
iridescent, holographic, color shifting, rainbow surface, oil slick colors, prismatic, pearlescent, opalescent, mother of pearl
