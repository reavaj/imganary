# Desaturated

Color drained toward gray — the bleach bypass of Saving Private Ryan, the somber palette of war photography and grief.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Reduces color saturation across the image, pulling hues toward neutral gray and creating a muted, documentary, or emotionally drained quality.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Colors present but weakened, ghostly
  - Gray undertone to everything
  - Somber, serious, documentary feel
  - Skin tones pallid and drained
  - Remaining color hints feel precious
- **Reference points:** Saving Private Ryan (bleach bypass), Schindler's List, war photography, Steven Soderbergh (Traffic), A24 indie films
- **Pairs well with:** gritty, melancholic, stark, grainy

## FLUX keywords
desaturated, muted colors, drained color, bleach bypass, low saturation, washed out color, subdued palette, faded color
