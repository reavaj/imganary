# Bleached

Color washed out by sun or chemical processing — faded California surf photography, sun-damaged posters, expired film.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Strips color down to ghostly remnants, as if the image has been left in the sun for years, creating a faded, memory-like, wistful quality.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Pale, ghostly remnants of former color
  - Whites and light tones dominate
  - Sun-damaged, aged, impermanent feeling
  - Memory-like, distant, unreachable
  - Warm undertone from UV degradation
- **Reference points:** Faded California surf/skate zines, sun-damaged posters, expired film photography, William Eggleston faded prints
- **Pairs well with:** nostalgic, overexposed, desolate, grainy

## FLUX keywords
bleached, sun faded, washed out, color drained, pale, sun bleached, faded photograph, light damaged, overbleached
