# Jewel-toned

Deep, rich, saturated gemstone colors — stained glass, Klimt's gold and emerald, Byzantine mosaics, velvet.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Saturates colors to their deepest, richest values — not bright like neon but deep like looking into a gemstone, creating opulence and visual weight.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Deep emerald, ruby, sapphire, amethyst hues
  - Rich saturation at medium-to-dark values
  - Luxurious, heavy, precious feeling
  - Often paired with gold accents
  - Stained glass luminosity when backlit
- **Reference points:** Stained glass windows, Klimt (The Kiss), Byzantine mosaics, Renaissance velvet, Art Nouveau jewelry
- **Pairs well with:** opulent, ornate, saturated, burnished

## FLUX keywords
jewel tones, emerald green, ruby red, sapphire blue, deep gemstone colors, stained glass luminosity, amethyst purple, heavy saturated hues at dark values, gold accents
