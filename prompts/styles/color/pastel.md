# Pastel

Soft, light, chalky colors with white mixed in — Wes Anderson's candy worlds, 1950s illustration, Rococo ceilings.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Lightens and softens every color by adding white, creating a gentle, low-contrast palette that feels nostalgic, youthful, or dreamlike.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - High value (lightness) across all colors
  - Low saturation — soft, not intense
  - Candy-colored, confectionery quality
  - Gentle, non-threatening, approachable
  - Chalky, matte feeling to surfaces
- **Reference points:** Wes Anderson (The Grand Budapest Hotel), 1950s magazine illustration, Rococo painting, cotton candy, Laduree macarons
- **Pairs well with:** whimsical, nostalgic, soft-focus, diffused

## FLUX keywords
pastel colors, soft colors, light colors, candy colored, pale tones, gentle palette, baby colors, chalky colors, muted bright
