# Neon

Electric, fluorescent, eye-searing synthetic color — Refn's hot pink, cyberpunk's toxic glow, 1980s nightlife.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Introduces unnaturally intense, fluorescent colors that appear to glow with their own light, creating an artificial, electrified, and often nocturnal atmosphere.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Colors appear self-luminous, glowing from within
  - Hot pink, electric blue, toxic green dominate
  - Dark backgrounds required to make neon pop
  - Artificial, synthetic, man-made light sources
  - Nightclub, city-at-night, underground energy
- **Reference points:** Nicolas Winding Refn (Drive, Only God Forgives), cyberpunk, 1980s nightlife photography, rave culture, Dan Flavin
- **Pairs well with:** harsh, graphic, hallucinatory, underexposed

## FLUX keywords
neon colors, neon glow, electric colors, fluorescent, glowing neon, hot pink, electric blue, neon signs, bright neon against dark
