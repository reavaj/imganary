# Muted

Subdued, grayed-down colors suggesting restraint and sophistication — A24 indie palettes, Japanese wabi-sabi, Scandinavian design.

## Visual DNA
- **What it does:** Adds gray to every color, pulling intensity down while maintaining distinct hues, creating an understated, quiet palette that whispers rather than shouts.
- **Key characteristics:**
  - Colors identifiable but softened, never loud
  - Gray mixed into every hue
  - Sophisticated, restrained, adult
  - Earth tones and sage greens feel natural here
  - Quiet visual rhythm, no jarring transitions
- **Reference points:** A24 films (Moonlight, Lady Bird), Japanese wabi-sabi, Scandinavian interiors, Agnes Varda, muted vintage fashion
- **Pairs well with:** austere, serene, minimal, diffused

## FLUX keywords
muted colors, subdued palette, quiet colors, understated, grayed colors, soft muted, toned down, earth tones muted
